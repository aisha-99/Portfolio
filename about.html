About Me!

Hi there! I’m Aisha, a graphic designer with a passion for creating fresh, vibrant designs that inspire. I specialize in branding, editorial design, packaging, and web design. I use my work to blend my creativity and functionality, creating designs that tell a story and leave a lasting impression.

With a sharp eye for detail and a playful design style, I create work that feels approachable and unique. Whether it’s crafting a striking brand identity, an engaging digital experience, or eco-friendly packaging, I bring a mix of creativity, precision, and adaptability to every project.

Besides design, I love all things sweet—especially strawberries, my go-to inspiration! When I’m not working on my next creative project, you can find me exploring the city for new ideas and design trends, reading novels, playing the latest mobile games or enjoying a strawberry matcha.

Whatever project comes my way, I make sure to bring passion, creativity, and a sprinkle of sweetness to ensure every design is as unique as you are.
